Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How quickly can I get a freight quote?

We respond to all quote requests within 2 business hours. For urgent shipments, call our 24/7 hotline for immediate assistance.

Can you handle dangerous or hazardous goods?

Yes. We are IATA DGR certified for air freight and handle ADR-compliant road transport, as well as IMDG-classified sea cargo.

Do you provide door-to-door delivery?

Absolutely. We offer complete door-to-door solutions across all modes — air, sea, and land — including customs clearance and last-mile delivery.

How can I track my shipment?

All clients receive a AdTrans tracking number. You can monitor your shipment 24/7 via our online portal or by contacting your account manager.

Which countries do you operate in?

We operate in 180+ countries through our global office network and trusted agent partnerships across every major trade lane.

Is cargo insurance included?

Basic liability coverage is included with every shipment. We also offer comprehensive all-risk marine cargo insurance — ask your account manager for details.
