Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust
in Global Logistics

Adtrans Logistics, Inc. is a Philippine-based freight forwarding company offering customized logistics for both Import and Export shipments, via Air and Sea.

Our Leadership team brings over 40 years of experience in logistics, with in depth expertise in International freight forwarding, Customs regulations, and Port operations.

We provide seamless, efficient, and cost-effective services tailored to the unique needs of each client, from Small businesses to multinational enterprises.
